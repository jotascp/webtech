ABOUT US

OUR MISSION
Programming Online Courses Ltd. offers free online courses through an “open-based” platform system where our users are challenged to contribute actively on different threads in which the main goal is to solve certain real-world problems.
Twice a year, a meeting takes place in the UK to promote this group and the connection among our members. This event consists on a 24 hour of continuing challenges and discoveries. Interested in making part of this amazing group? Get in touch with us on the Contact tab!
